Vaibhav Taneja has filed a Form 144 to sell 3,000 shares of Tesla (TSLA) common stock. The proposed sale, which is for restricted stock, has a transaction date of May 13, 2026, and is expected to generate $1,350,000.00. A Form 144 is a notice of intent to sell restricted securities and does not confirm the actual sale has occurred.

What Happens If Tesla and SpaceX Merge?
Morningstar analysts examine the potential for a merger between Tesla and SpaceX, driven by Elon Musk's desire for consolidation and increasing AI collaboration between the companies. While a merger presents strong business cases like streamlined operations and shared AI development, significant hurdles exist, including divergent long-term goals, regulatory concerns, and especially the challenge of agreeing on valuation and equity distribution that satisfies both Tesla and SpaceX shareholders, given SpaceX's high valuation multiples. They expect discussions to intensify after SpaceX's IPO.
