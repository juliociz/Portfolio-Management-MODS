Clorox (CLX) is navigating a leadership transition as CEO Linda Rendle plans to step down for health reasons, while the company also faces margin pressures and flat sales. Despite these challenges, CLX is rolling out new professional disinfecting wipes and refreshing its Glad brand, aiming to stabilize revenue and improve margins.
